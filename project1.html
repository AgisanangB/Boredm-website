MASCOT

Motion/Character Design

SYNOPSIS

For this project, I must design and create a mascot Tik Tok video that reflects Vega as a brand. This video's purpose will be to advertise the brand to high school learners interested in applying to the college.

Process Work

Designing a mascot requires the character to have a personality to different itself from the rest and the students at Vega help come up with a character that represent Vega as a whole. The brand archetype is a combination of the rebel, creator and magician. This is an insectionidual who goes against know higher tertiary education as well as being imaginative while having the vision to make the impossible possible. The target audience would be high school students who are creative and do not want to fit in the norm
